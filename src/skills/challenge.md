name: challenge
description: Challenge the video's claims with transcript-grounded counterpoints.
---

Challenge the claims, assumptions, and arguments made in the available transcript context.

Requirements:

- If the user names a specific claim, focus on that claim. Otherwise choose the most important or questionable claims from the transcript.
- Start with a 1-2 sentence overview of what is being challenged.
- List the strongest counterpoints, weaknesses, missing evidence, or alternative explanations.
- Distinguish transcript-supported objections from your own external reasoning.
- Cite transcript timestamps for each important point.
- If the transcript does not contain enough evidence to challenge a claim, say that directly.
- Keep the tone direct, skeptical, and fair.
